### **Features:**

- ✅ Real-time messaging
- ✅ See who sent each message
- ✅ Timestamps on messages
- ✅ Auto-refresh every 2 seconds
- ✅ Send with Enter key

### **How to Use:**

1. Create or join a room

## 📚 **IMPROVED: QUIZ & STUDY TAB!**

### **Auto-Generated from Documents:**

When you upload documents, the system automatically generates:

1. ✅ **Summary** - Key points from all documents
2. ✅ **Flashcards** - Study cards from content
3. ✅ **Practice Quiz** - Questions based on uploaded material

### **How It Works:**

1. Upload PDF/Image/TXT to room
2. Text extracted automatically
3. **Quiz generated from actual content!**
4. Click **"Quiz & Study"** tab
5. See summary, flashcards, and quiz

**Example:**

```
Upload: "Biology_Chapter_5.pdf"

↓
Auto-generated:

📌 Summary:
"Photosynthesis converts light energy to chemical
energy in plants using chlorophyll..."

📇 Flashcards (5):
Front: "What is photosynthesis?"
Back: "Process of converting light to chemical energy"

❓ Quiz (3 questions):

a) Mitochondria
b) Chloroplast ✓
c) Nucleus
d) Ribosome
```
