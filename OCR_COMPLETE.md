## 🚀 **HOW TO USE:**

### **Upload Images with Text:**

1. Go to **Notebook**
2. Click **"New Notebook"**
3. Click **"Upload Sources"**
4. Select an **image file** (PNG/JPG)
5. **OCR extracts text automatically!**

**Example:**

```
Upload: screenshot.png (contains text)
↓
OCR Processing... 25%... 50%... 75%... 100%
↓
✅ Extracted 120 words from "screenshot.png"

Content:
"This is the text that was in the image..."
```

## 🌐 **BROWSER EXTENSION:**

### **Install Now:**

1. **Open Chrome/Edge:**
   - Go to `chrome://extensions/` or `edge://extensions/`

2. **Enable Developer Mode:**
   - Toggle ON (top right corner)

## 💡 **HOW OCR WORKS:**

### **Process:**

1. **Upload image/scanned PDF**
2. **System detects it's an image**
3. **Tesseract.js OCR starts**
4. **Progress shown: 0%... 25%... 50%... 75%... 100%**
5. **Text extracted**
6. **Content auto-filled**

**Console Logs:**

```
Starting OCR extraction for: notes.png
OCR Progress: 25%
OCR Progress: 50%
OCR Progress: 75%
OCR Progress: 100%
OCR extraction complete. Length: 450
✅ Extracted 90 words from "notes.png"
```
