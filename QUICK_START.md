## 🎯 How to Use

### **Step 1: Access the Application**

Open your browser and go to: **http://localhost:5173**

### **Step 2: Create an Account**

1. Click **"Sign Up"** on the login page
2. Enter:
   - Your full name
   - Email address
   - Password (min 6 characters)
   - Confirm password
3. Click **"Create Account"**

### **Step 3: Start Studying**

1. You'll see the **Dashboard** with:
   - Personalized greeting
   - Study timer
   - Stats overview
   - Progress ring
   - Study graph
2. Click **"Start Session"** on the timer
3. Study for any duration
4. Click **"Stop"** to save and earn XP + badges!

### **Step 4: Explore Features**

#### **📅 Planner**

- Click "Planner" in navigation
- Click "Add Exam"
- Enter subject, date, and topics
- Get auto-generated daily study plan!

#### **👥 Study Rooms**

- Click "Study Rooms" in navigation
- Create a room or join with a code
- Chat with study partners in real-time

#### **📝 Notebook**

- Click "Notebook" in navigation
- Create notes with subjects
- Search and organize your learning

#### **👤 Profile**

- Click "Profile" in navigation
- View all your stats and achievements
- See focus patterns and badges

#### **🤖 AI Chatbot**

- Click the 💬 icon (bottom-right)
- Ask for study tips, progress, or help
- Use voice input with the 🎤 button
- Click "Solve Doubt" for subject help

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### **Issue: Timer not working**

**Solution**: Refresh the page and try again

### **Issue: Data not saving**

**Solution**: Check if localStorage is enabled in your browser

### **Issue: Voice assistant not responding**

**Solution**:

- Use Chrome or Edge
- Grant microphone permissions
- Check browser console for errors

### **Issue: Graphs not showing**

**Solution**: Complete at least one study session first
