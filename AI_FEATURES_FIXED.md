## 🔧 **TECHNICAL DETAILS**

### **AI Functions Created:**

1. **generateSummary(text)**: Extracts first 3 sentences
2. **generateFAQs(text, title)**: Creates 4 Q&A pairs
3. **generateFlashcards(text)**: Makes study cards from keywords
4. **generateQuiz(text, title)**: Creates multiple choice questions
5. **generateTimeline(text)**: Breaks content into steps
6. **generateMindMap(text)**: Shows concept connections
7. **generatePodcastScript(text, title)**: Audio overview info
8. **getChatbotResponse(message, context)**: Smart chat replies
9. **solveStudyDoubt(question, subject)**: Subject-specific help

### **How It Works:**

1. **Keyword Extraction**:
   - Removes common words (the, a, and, etc.)
   - Counts word frequency
   - Returns top concepts

2. **Summary Generation**:
   - Splits text into sentences
   - Takes first 2-3 sentences
   - Returns as summary

3. **FAQ Generation**:
   - Analyzes content
   - Creates relevant questions
   - Provides answers from text

4. **Flashcard Creation**:
   - Uses top keywords
   - Finds sentences with keywords
   - Creates Q&A pairs

5. **Quiz Generation**:
   - Multiple choice from keywords
   - True/False from sentences
   - Fill in the blank
