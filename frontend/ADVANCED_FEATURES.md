## 📅 **2. Advanced Study Planner**

### **🎯 Two Powerful Modes:**

### **Mode 1: Exam Preparation** 📝

**What You Create:**

- Subject name
- Exam date
- Topics (comma-separated)
- Hours per day (1-6)
- Current level (beginner/intermediate/advanced)

**What You Get:**

- ✅ **Day-by-Day Timeline** from Day 1 to Exam Day
- ✅ **Three Learning Phases**:
  - 📚 **Learning Phase** (70% of time)
    - Focus on understanding
    - Take detailed notes
    - Practice basics
    - Watch tutorials
  - ⚡ **Practice Phase** (20% of time)
    - Solve problems
    - Review previous topics
    - Speed up solving
    - Take quizzes
  - 🔍 **Revision Phase** (10% of time)
    - Quick revision
    - Review notes
    - Focus on weak areas
    - Stay confident

- ✅ **Daily Breakdown**:
  - Date for each day
  - Topics to cover
  - Hours to study
  - Phase-specific tips
  - Study suggestions

**Example Exam Plan:**

```
Subject: Mathematics
Exam: Feb 20, 2024
Topics: Algebra, Calculus, Geometry, Trigonometry
Hours/Day: 3 hours
Level: Intermediate

Result: 20-day timeline with:
- Days 1-14: Learning Phase
- Days 15-18: Practice Phase
- Days 19-20: Revision Phase
```

---

### **Mode 2: Topic Learning** 📚

**What You Create:**

- Topic name
- Difficulty level (beginner/intermediate/advanced)
- Target days (7/14/21/30)
- Hours per day (1-6)

**What You Get:**

- ✅ **Complete Learning Timeline**
- ✅ **Three Mastery Phases**:
  - 🌱 **Foundation** (30% of time)
    - Read introductory materials
    - Understand terminology
    - Identify key concepts
    - Create concept map
  - 🔬 **Deep Dive** (40% of time)
    - Study advanced concepts
    - Work through examples
    - Solve practice problems
    - Watch expert explanations
  - 🏆 **Mastery** (30% of time)
    - Practice complex problems
    - Review and reinforce
    - Test your knowledge
    - Apply to real scenarios

- ✅ **Daily Activities**:
  - Specific tasks for each day
  - Phase milestones
  - Progress tracking
  - Learning objectives

**Example Topic Plan:**

```
Topic: Machine Learning
Difficulty: Intermediate
Days: 14 days
Hours/Day: 2 hours

Result:
- Days 1-4: Foundation (basics, terminology)
- Days 5-10: Deep Dive (advanced concepts)
- Days 11-14: Mastery (complex problems)
```

## 🎨 **How to Use**

### **📚 Notebook (NotebookLM-style):**

1. **Create Notebook**:
   - Click "New Notebook"
   - Enter title and subject
   - Upload files (optional)
   - Paste/write content
   - Click "Create Notebook"

2. **Analyze Content**:
   - Click on any notebook from left sidebar
   - Wait 2 seconds for AI analysis
   - View results in tabs:
     - **Chat**: Summary + FAQs
     - **Studio**: Flashcards, quiz, mind map, podcast
     - **Sources**: Original content

3. **Features to Try**:
   - Upload PDFs/docs
   - Get auto-summary
   - Review FAQs
   - Study with flashcards
   - Take practice quiz
   - Explore mind map

---

### **📅 Study Planner:**

#### **For Exam Prep:**

1. Click "Create Plan"
2. Select "📝 Exam Preparation"
3. Fill in:
   - Subject: "Physics"
   - Exam Date: Pick date
   - Topics: "Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Optics"
   - Hours/Day: 3
   - Level: Intermediate
4. Click "Generate Plan"
5. Click "View Day-by-Day Timeline"
6. See complete plan with:
   - Each day's topics
   - Learning phase
   - Study tips
   - Time allocation

#### **For Topic Learning:**

1. Click "Create Plan"
2. Select "📚 Topic Learning"
3. Fill in:
   - Topic: "React Development"
   - Difficulty: Intermediate
   - Days: 14
   - Hours/Day: 2
4. Click "Generate Plan"
5. Click "View Day-by-Day Timeline"
6. See complete plan with:
   - Foundation → Deep Dive → Mastery
   - Daily activities
   - Milestones
   - Progress markers
