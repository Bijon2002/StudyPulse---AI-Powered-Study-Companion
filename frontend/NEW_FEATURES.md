## 🎯 **How to Use**

### **Notebook with AI:**

1. **Go to Notebook page**
2. **Click "New Note"**
3. **Add title, subject, and content**
4. **Save the note**
5. **Click the 🧠 Brain icon** on any note
6. **View AI analysis** in the right panel:
   - Summary
   - Insights
   - Key concepts

### **Study Plan Creator:**

1. **Go to Planner page**
2. **Click "Create Plan"**
3. **Fill in the form:**
   - Subject: "Mathematics"
   - Exam Date: Select date
   - Topics: "Algebra, Calculus, Geometry"
   - Hours/Day: Select 2-6 hours
4. **Click "Create Plan"**
5. **View your plan** with:
   - Days until exam
   - Total hours needed
   - Topics to cover
   - Daily recommendations
